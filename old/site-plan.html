Education

At Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah, I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from June 2022 to December 2023. During my time there, I built a solid foundation in programming principles, data structures, and algorithms, while also gaining hands-on experience with project-based learning. My coursework included advanced topics in computer science that enhanced my problem-solving skills and prepared me for more specialized work in software development. This experience was pivotal in shaping my technical abilities and sparking my interest in development.

Currently, I am continuing my studies toward a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Brigham Young University-Idaho in Rexburg, Idaho. Starting in August 2024, I have been able to deepen my knowledge of software engineering principles and gain additional practical experience in areas like database management, application development, and teamwork within technical environments. My education here is complemented by a focus on collaborative learning, which has strengthened my ability to work effectively within development teams.

In the summer of 2024, I completed the CodePath Technical Interview Course, where I developed essential skills for technical interviews. The course focused on data structures, algorithms, and problem-solving strategies, helping me improve my coding efficiency and preparing me to approach complex coding challenges with confidence. This experience strengthened my technical foundation and equipped me with practical strategies for succeeding in competitive technical assessments.
